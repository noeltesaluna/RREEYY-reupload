ABOUT

RREEYY was formed in early 2018. We formed after Tyler Vo Doan signed us to a music room booking at our school. It was at this time that the band did not know what to play. So for the first few days we slacked off and screamed into the mics and chilled out in the music room. However, one day the teacher on duty caught us and told us to actually start practicing or we would be kicked off. Because of this we decided to learn a few basic songs with different genres. However, when we first played pop punk, our band clicked. We then preformed a bunch and we decided that we didnt want to be a cover band, so we started making our own songs that havent been released yet.
